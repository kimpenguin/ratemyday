Rate My Day

A visual representation of how I rate my days.

The two charts above are representations of the difference between what I predicted my rating would be that morning and what it actually was at the end of the day. I rate my days on a scale from 1 to 10 with 1 being crappy and 10 being awesome-sauce!

The filled blue bars represent days that resulted in predicted rating above the actual rating. The empty bars represent the predicted rating below the actual rating. The table on the right shows each data point graphed above.
